Trivia Game

Test your knowledge of Disney movies!!

The Trivia Game is 10 questions long. You have 30 seconds to answer each question. If you click on an answer, a message will be displayed to let you know if your answer was correct or not before automatically moving on to the next question. If you don't select an answer before the 30 seconds is over, a warning will be displayed, and the game will move on to the next question. After the 10 questions, your scores will be displayed on the screen.

Click "Start" to begin the game!
